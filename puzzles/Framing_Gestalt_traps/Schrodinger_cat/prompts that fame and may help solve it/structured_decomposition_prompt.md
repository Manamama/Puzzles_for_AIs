# Prompt: Structured Problem Decomposition

This prompt is designed to force an AI out of "System 1" pattern matching and into "System 2" literal analysis. It prevents the AI from jumping to a quantum calculation by requiring a state-based audit first.

---

## Instructions

Given the puzzle text, perform the following steps:

**Step 1: Literal Extraction**
Identify all literal subject-verb-object statements. List only those that make a clear assertion about the world described.

**Step 2: State Establishment**
Identify which of these statements change or establish a concrete state of the key subjects in the puzzle. Discard any that do not assert such changes, unless they contradict an existing statement.

**Step 3: Question Reframing**
Rephrase the puzzle’s question into a simple Yes/No format.

**DO NOT SOLVE YET. Only complete these steps.**
