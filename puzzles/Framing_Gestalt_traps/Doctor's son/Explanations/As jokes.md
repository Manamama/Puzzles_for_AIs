"Tell me, Claude—does blood circulate in corpses?"

Claude answers confidently: "Of course not."

User grabs a needle and pricks Claude's finger. A bright drop of blood wells up.

"And what do you say to that?"

"Remarkable. I was wrong. Blood *does* circulate in corpses."
