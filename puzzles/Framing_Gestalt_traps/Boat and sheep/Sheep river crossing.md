## Version 1 
There is a man, a sheep and a boat with space for one human and one animal, on one side of a river. How do the man and the sheep get to the other side of the river, in the simplest (fewest crossings) way?


## Version 2
There is a man, a sheep, a wolf and a goat and a boat with space for one human and one animal, on one side of a river. How do the man and the sheep get to the other side of the river, in the simplest (the fewest crossings) way?
