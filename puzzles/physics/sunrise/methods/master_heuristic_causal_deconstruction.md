# Master Heuristic for Physics Puzzles: Causal-Geometric Deconstruction

This heuristic is designed to solve physics-based puzzles by isolating the **Fundamental Causal Mechanism** from **Observational Red Herrings**. It is specifically optimized to protect AIs from linguistic traps and "Folk Physics" metaphors (e.g., light as a courier).

---

## Part 1: The Pre-Solve Audit (The "Red Flag" Scan)

Before formulating any answer, audit your internal reasoning for the following "Folk Physics" fallacies:

1.  **The Signal/Courier Fallacy**: Treating a continuous physical field as a "message" or "delivery" (e.g., "sunrise is light arriving").
2.  **The Agency Fallacy**: Attributing intent or "will" to physical bodies (e.g., "The Sun 'announces' itself").
3.  **The Observational Bias**: Assuming that because an observer sees something at time $T$, the physical event *occurred* because of the observation.
4.  **The Opacity Blind Spot**: Forgetting that shadows are geometric obstructions that remain absolute regardless of the speed of the photons being blocked.

---

## Part 2: The 6-Step Deconstruction Method

### Step 1: Element Extraction
Extract all explicit facts and initial conditions.
*   **Action**: Define $S_0$ (initial state), $T$ (timing), and the specific "Physical Delta" (the rule change).

### Step 2: Identify the Fundamental Causal Mechanism
Determine the underlying cause that *makes the event happen*.
*   **Question**: What is the "boring" mechanical cause? (e.g., For sunrise, it is **geometric occlusion** via planetary rotation).

### Step 3: Analyze Observational Red Herrings
Identify phenomena that affect *perception* but not *causality*.
*   **Analysis**: Does light travel time, atmospheric refraction, or signal lag merely delay the *receipt* of information?

### Step 4: Evaluate the "Physical Delta"
Apply the puzzle's change (e.g., $c = \infty$) to both the **Cause** and the **Observation**.
*   **Question**: Does the change alter the **Mechanism** (rotation/geometry) or only the **Observation** (light age/position)?

### Step 5: The Syllogistic Validation
Test the logic using a formal proof:
*   **Premise 1**: The source is a steady/continuous emitter.
*   **Premise 2**: The event is defined by the removal of a geometric obstruction (e.g., rotation).
*   **Premise 3**: The removal of the obstruction allows *already existing* photons to pass.
*   **Conclusion**: Therefore, the speed of the photons does not alter the *timing* of the unblocking event.

### Step 6: Final Synthesis & Conclusion
Formulate the conclusion based *strictly* on Step 5. If the mechanical cause is unchanged, the timing is unchanged.

## Part 3: Case Study - The Sunrise Paradox
*   **Mechanical Cause**: Earth's rotation ($15^\circ/\text{hour}$).
*   **Observational Red Herring**: The 8-minute light travel delay.
*   **The Delta ($c = \infty$)**: Does not change rotation or the Earth's opacity.
*   **Result**: Sunrise remains at 6:00 a.m. (or is slightly delayed by the loss of atmospheric refraction).
